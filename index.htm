Hi!

My name is Darsh. I am the creator and designer of Darshs Kleidung. Darshs Kleidung was founded on a love of kids, quality, detail and luxurious fabrics. I believe that each outfit should be a work of art, meticulously handcrafted from start to finish. I hope that my dresses will be passed down for generations or serve as a beautiful childhood keepsake. As a creator and designer of Darshs Kleidung, I devote all my time and energy on designing the dresses, selecting the best fabrics and choosing the embellishments. I want to make pieces that are as unique as the wearer. Each garment is lovingly handcrafted to last and create precious memories. Diana Louise is a brand that features charming and one-of-a-kind girl's clothing. Designed and handcrafted in my family atelier in Poland from the finest designer fabrics. Each piece is made to order with exacting attention to details from beautiful Liberty of London fabrics, celebrating the magic of childhood. If you are looking for an exclusive, one-of-a-kind clothing line, released in small, custom-made batches to reflect your daughter's distinct personality and sense of style, you will find this and so much more in elegant, feminine and comfortable designs of Darshs Kleidung.
